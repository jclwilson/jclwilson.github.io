Back in October 2016 I covered <cite>Landfill Edition</cite>'s exhibition <cite>[Mould Map 6: TERRAFORMERS](http://www.thedoublenegative.co.uk/2016/11/visualising-our-horrors-of-dystopia-introducing-mould-map-magazine/)</cite> for <cite>[The Double Negative](http://www.thedoublenegative.co.uk/2016/11/visualising-our-horrors-of-dystopia-introducing-mould-map-magazine/)</cite>, Jaakko Pallasvuo's <cite>Easy Rider</cite> was there in fragments, but has since been published in full. I recently talked to Pallasvuo about this work and his worries for the future.

> It’s a very confusing time, and in some ways it feels like simply being human has become a fraught, contradictory and outmoded position. This is frightening because I can’t really see a good way out. Looking for solutions turns into thinking about the supernatural.
