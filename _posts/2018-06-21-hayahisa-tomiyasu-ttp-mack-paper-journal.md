In May Hayahisa Tomiyasu was awarded the First Book Prize 2018 by [MACK](http://www.mackbooks.co.uk/). It's an excellent book, a story made from the photographic acts of repetition and editing to build an emotional story of a table and a community out of a set of supposed coincidences.

> The mythology of the camera is that of capturing coincidences—some photographers may even go as far as to fake the coincidence to make a better picture—the suggestion of chance makes a better story, the unexpected and surreal coincidences are those that are best remembered, and best told.
