On a foggy February morning I went for an early cycle around the Southbank and saw Wolfgang Tillmans' latest self-titled show at Tate Modern, London.

> Despite the many claims of objectivity, photography is a medium of doubt; Tillmans doesn’t attempt to give an entirely accurate experience, just a partial one, as much as one man with a camera can.
