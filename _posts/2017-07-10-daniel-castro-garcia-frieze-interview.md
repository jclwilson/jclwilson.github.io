I recently spoke with [Daniel Castro Garcia](http://www.danielcastrogarcia.com/) about his work that's trying to change the perception of migrants, refugees, & asylum seekers arriving in Europe. Daniel has a new show opening this week at <cite>[Cortona On The Move](http://www.cortonaonthemove.com/)</cite>, Italy – I highly recommend going and seeing it if possible.

> I don't see myself finishing working on this subject any time soon. I want to carry on with it and I think it's a situation that's only going to increase with global warming, which is going to result in big migratory movements of people – not just to Europe but in the Americas and Asia too. Everywhere is affected.
