In October last year I drunkenly wrote into Viz' letterbocks. I didn't think anything of it until I opened up the March issue to see my rambling joke printed in the corner of page 9.

> That Kim Jong-Un might think he's all that for developing a working nuclear weapons programme despite international pressure to stop, but I don't think he's so clever. Whenever he's in the news he's always wearing his coat indoors, so he clearly won't feel the benefit when he goes out later
