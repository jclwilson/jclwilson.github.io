Since 2012, the photography publishers MACK have sought to support the work of emerging and unpublished artists with their First Book Award. The prize has helped further the careers of numerous photographers, In 2018 [I wrote on](https://jacobcharleswilson.com/hayahisa-tomiyasu-ttp-mack-paper-journal) winner Hayahisa Tomiyasu and his book _TTP_ for [Paper Journal](https://paper-journal.com/hayahisa-tomiyasu-ttp/). Even appearing on the shortlist can help artists make their name. I rounded up the shortlisted publications and artists of this year's award for _AnOther_.
