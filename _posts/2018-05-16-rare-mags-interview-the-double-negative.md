After the joy of reading about Rare Mags opening in Stockport, I had the pleasure of talking to co-founder Martin Wilson to hear more about his story, his interest in publishing, and the shop that he, together with Holly Carter, has just opened.

> We're living in a good time for print. It’s definitely become a lot more accessible just in terms of getting these things printed. It's no longer one publisher ruling the roost and producing 50 different titles, it's independents working together and coming out of all sorts of places.
