I'll be working at [The Modern Magazine 2017](https://magculture.com/the-modern-magazine-17/) this Thursday, covering the day's events for the [_magCulture Journal_](https://magculture.com/). So, this week I thought I'd highlight a few magazines that I've enjoyed recently.

- [_Buffalo Zine_](http://buffalozine.com/). I utterly adore the playfulness of this magazine, which redesigns itself for every single issue. The current issue sees six different covers, each a pastiche of the kind of in-house supermarket magazine that I always find on my parents' kitchen counter.
- [_Real Review_](https://real-review.org/). For the past few years Jack Self and the [Real Foundation](http://real.foundation/) have made a serious effort of reimagining of what a magazine could look like, feel like, and change in the world.
- [_Somesuch Stories_](https://somesuchstories.co/). I subscribed to their website in early summer but I've not yet picked up a print copy. Their mixture of fiction and factual makes me think of it as a smaller version of the [London Review of Books](https://www.lrb.co.uk/).
- [_Sabat Magazine_](http://www.sabatmagazine.com/). It was only in June that I first saw Sabat's 'Crone Issue'. Their design reflects the myserty and hidden knowledges held by the figure of the crone.
- [_Gut Magazine_](http://www.gutmagazine.com/). Taking a more playful side of the gross & esoteric.
- [Stack Awards Shortlist](https://www.stackmagazines.com/awards/). Also coming up soon are [The Stack Awards 2017](https://www.stackmagazines.com/awards-2017/), one of the best places to start when looking for notable independent magazines. I could list every one of the shortlisted publications here, but they've already done the hard work.
