I reviewed the joint show of Edouard Malingue and Mother's Tankstation at CONDO London 2018 for <cite>ArtAsiaPacific</cite>.

> In recent years, London art-goers have become used to ad hoc exhibition spaces thrown together in the cracks and crevices of the city. Thus, a former office-space on the fourth floor of a condemned tower block isn’t the strangest place to find a co-curated exhibition of works from two up-and-coming international galleries—Edouard Malingue Gallery and Mother’s Tankstation
