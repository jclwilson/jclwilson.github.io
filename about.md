I'm a freelance arts writer focusing on photography, publishing, and print design. I studied Art History at [The Courtauld Institute of Art](https://courtauld.ac.uk) and I've worked at [<cite>Tank Magazine</cite>](https://tankmagazine.com), the [<cite>ICA</cite>](https://www.ica.art), and the [<cite>Delfina Foundation</cite>](http://www.delfinafoundation.com/). I'm currently the Editorial Assistant at [<cite>Paper Journal</cite>](https://paper-journal.com/).

My work's been featured by [<cite>Huck</cite>](https://www.huckmag.com), [<cite>AnOther</cite>](http://www.anothermag.com/), [<cite>The Plant</cite>](http://theplantmagazine.com/), [<cite>Elephant</cite>](https://elephant.art/), [<cite>Somesuch Stories</cite>](http://somesuchstories.co), [<cite>Above Sea Level</cite>](http://www.above-sea-level.co/), [<cite>ArtAsiaPacific</cite>](http://artasiapacific.com/), the [<cite>magCulture Journal</cite>](https://magculture.com), [<cite>Eye Magazine</cite>](https://www.eyemagazine.com/), [<cite>It's Nice That</cite>](https://itsnicethat.com), [<cite>Port Magazine</cite>](http://port-magazine.com), <cite><a href="https://frieze.com">Frieze</a></cite>, [<cite>AIGA Eye on Design</cite>](https://eyeondesign.aiga.org), [<cite>The London Magazine</cite>](https://www.thelondonmagazine.org/), and [<cite>The Double Negative</cite>](http://www.thedoublenegative.co.uk).

I've written copy for [<cite>Because London</cite>](http://becauselondon.com) and [<cite>Together Associates</cite>](http://www.togetherassociates.com/), and I've helped a number of arts organisations send newsletters. I've also coded a few websites, including the [RCA Poster Archive](https://rca-poster-archive.co.uk/), as well as [this site](https://github.com/jclwilson/jclwilson.github.io).
