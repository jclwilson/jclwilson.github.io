I'm a freelance arts writer focusing on photography, publishing, and print design. I studied Art History at [The Courtauld Institute of Art](https://courtauld.ac.uk) and I've worked at <cite>[Tank Magazine](https://tankmagazine.com)</cite>, the <cite>[ICA](https://www.ica.art)</cite>, and the <cite>[Delfina Foundation](http://www.delfinafoundation.com/)</cite>.

I contribute to <cite><a href="http://www.thedoublenegative.co.uk">The Double Negative</a></cite>, and my work's been featured by <cite><a href="https://www.huckmag.com">Huck</a></cite>, <cite><a href="http://www.anothermag.com/">AnOther</a></cite>, <cite><a href="https://www.eyemagazine.com/">Eye Magazine</a></cite>, <cite><a href="https://elephant.art/">Elephant</a></cite>, <cite><a href="http://somesuchstories.co">Somesuch Stories</a></cite>, <cite><a href="http://artasiapacific.com/">ArtAsiaPacific</a></cite>, the <cite><a href="https://magculture.com">magCulture Journal</a></cite>, <cite><a href="https://itsnicethat.com">It's Nice That</a></cite>, <cite><a href="http://port-magazine.com">Port Magazine</a></cite>, <cite><a href="https://frieze.com">Frieze</a></cite>, <cite><a href="https://eyeondesign.aiga.org">AIGA Eye on Design</a></cite>, <cite><a href="https://www.thelondonmagazine.org/">The London Magazine</a></cite>, <cite><a href="http://theplantmagazine.com/">The Plant</a></cite>, and <cite><a href="http://paper-journal.com/">Paper Journal</a></cite>.</p>

I've written copy for <cite>[Because London](http://becauselondon.com)</cite> and [<cite>Together Associates</cite>](http://www.togetherassociates.com/), and I've helped a number of arts organisations send newsletters. I've also coded a few websites, including the [RCA Poster Archive](https://rca-poster-archive.co.uk/), as well as [this site](https://github.com/jclwilson/jclwilson.github.io).
