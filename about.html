I'm a freelance arts writer focusing on photography, publishing, and print design. I studied Art History at The Courtauld and I've worked at Tank Magazine, the ICA, and the Delfina Foundation.

I contribute to The Double Negative, and my work's been featured by Huck, AnOther, Eye Magazine, Elephant, Somesuch Stories, ArtAsiaPacific, the magCulture Journal, It's Nice That, Port Magazine, Frieze, AIGA Eye on Design, The London Magazine, The Plant, and Paper Journal.

I've written copy for Because London and Together Associates, and I've helped a number of arts organisations send newsletters. I've also coded a few websites, including the RCA Poster Archive, as well as this site.
